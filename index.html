Welcome to Gordo's detail and hand car wash

We are experts performing deep and detailed cleaning on all types of motorized vehicles. With our service you can ecpect:

  • We will get rid of seat stains and bad smells
  • Your car will get a shine making it look fresh and comfortable to drive
  • Deep cleaning will guarantee you'll not find any hidden trashes under the seats nor small compartments your vehicle might have

Feel free to contact us so we can have a look at your vehicle and give you a quote and recommendations of what services will fit best for you!

Services

Services

Our services include:

Hand polish

Your car paint gets a smooth hand polish to bring its shine back and to minimize most scratches

Odor and stain removal

Your seats get our special treatment which helps remove stains and bad odors

General interior detailing

We vacuum any dust and trash that might be present on your vehicle!

Engine cleaning

Your engine gets a special treatment that safely remove any grease stains and give a shiny look, this service is excellent previous any car shows where you'll want to show off your horsepower

Wheels detailing

Not only your wheels get a smooth clean, but also the surrounding areas where not many look into. Alongside the engine cleaning this is also an excellent choice previous any car shows.

Deep cleaning

We perform a 100% cleanup on your vehicle, even the most hidden areas are taken care off. This service is recommended also for car sellers who want to give the best impressions.
